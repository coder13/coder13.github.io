Anatomy of a Cube

Centers

The cube has 6 center pieces. They form a fixed frame around which the other pieces move. The color of the center sticker determines the color of the face.

Corners

A piece with three stickers. The cube has 8 corner pieces.

Edges

A piece with two stickers. It is solved when both stickers match their neighboring centre pieces. The cube has 12 edges in total.

Cross

Solve the 4 edges around the white center. Split up this step by first putting the 4 white edges around the yellow center making a "daisy", then solving them into their appropriate spot.

You may run into a case where an edge piece is flipped while making the daisy:

You will just preform the following moves to put it in place, start by holding the yellow center down and put the flipped edge ontop of where it belongs: U' R' F R

Bottom Corners

Hence forth, white should remain on the bottom.

(e.g.) the Blue-Red-White corner needs to be in between the blue and red centers.

Middle Edges

During this step, you will solve the 4 middle edges. The first step is to pick an edge to solve. Find it's front sticker, the one facing you, and match it with the center of the same color. Then figure out if the edge needs to go to the left spot or the right spot.

If the edge needs to go to the right spot.
(U R U' R')
spin clockwise
(U' L' U L)
If the edge needs to go to the left spot.
(U' L' U L)
spin counter-clockwise
(U R U' R')
